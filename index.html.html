About Adriana

A Compassionate Vision for Growth

Behavioral therapy is deeply personal. I founded Bamboo with a simple goal: to create a therapy environment that doesn't feel clinical, but rather like a nurturing extension of home.

My work is centered around the belief that when a child feels safe and understood, they can overcome any behavioral or mental obstacle.

Our Vision

To set a new standard for pediatric behavioral health in Miami, where every family feels supported and every child thrives.

The Bamboo Way

Safe Environment

Our facility exceeds safety standards, offering parents absolute peace of mind while their children learn and explore.

Positive Approach

We use evidence-based positive reinforcement to build confidence and sustainable behavioral improvements.

Family Synergy

Specialized Programs

Expertly curated services to ensure your child gets the best possible support.

ABA Therapy

Clinical Applied Behavior Analysis that focuses on meaningful skill acquisition in a fun, naturalistic setting.

Early Intervention

Identifying and addressing developmental delays early to give your child the strongest foundation for life.

Social Skills

Guided peer groups that help children navigate social complexities, build friendships, and improve communication.

Parent Coaching

Empowering you with the strategies and tools needed to support your child's unique developmental journey.
